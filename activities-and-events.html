ACTIVITIES AND EVENTS

ACTIVITIES AND EVENTS IN JASPER

Your go-to destination for the latest happenings and gatherings in Jasper. Whether you're seeking cultural experiences, community celebrations, or entertainment options, this page serves as a comprehensive guide to keep you informed and engaged. Explore upcoming events, discover diverse activities, and stay in the loop with our regularly updated calendar.
Join us on this exciting journey as we highlight the pulse of our community and beyond.

Ski and Snowboarding

Jasper provides opportunities for backcountry skiing and a range of stores where you can get all the equipment you need to hit the slopes!

Trail

Hiking, strolling, or bicycling are great ways to discover the marvels of Jasper National Park because there are so many route alternatives.

Adventure Tours

Surrounded by mountains, Jasper National Park is a wonderland of sparkling glaciers, crystal-clear lakes, thundering waterfalls, evergreen forest, and roaming wildlife. Explore Jasper's sights independently or by joining a knowledgeable guided tour.

Food Trip

Find your new favorite restaurant in Jasper by exploring the area's dining options, which range from filling mountain breakfasts to sophisticated alpine meals for two in quaint caf√©s and welcoming pubs.

Water Sports

Almost any type of water sport is allowed in the Jasper National Park, including swimming, scuba diving, boating, kayaking, canoeing, fishing, and whitewater rafting.

Wildlife Viewing

Caribou, elk, moose, bighorn sheep, mountain goats, bears, and wolves can all be found in the park. The spring and fall are the greatest seasons to watch wildlife. Keep in mind that feeding or handling wildlife is prohibited and dangerous.

Spa and Wellness

For rejuvenating and calming medical treatments, visit the spa in Jasper; you can leave feeling like you have a new body.

Arts and Culture

Visit Jasper's galleries and local museums to discover the creative side of the Canadian Rockies, or visit the planetarium to learn about the Dark Sky Preserve and the region's rich history.

Jasper Skytram

You may see breathtaking views of mountain ranges up to 80 kilometers away from Whistlers Mountain, where the Jasper SkyTram quickly ascends to 2,263 meters.
